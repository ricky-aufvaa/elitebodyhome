## Frequently Asked Questions
### How soon will I see results after my acne treatment?
Results vary depending on the treatment method and severity of the acne. Most clients notice improvements within a few weeks, while significant changes may take a couple of months. Our specialists will provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific treatment plan.
### Are acne treatments painful?
Most acne treatments, such as chemical peels or laser therapy, may cause mild discomfort, often described as a tingling or warming sensation. Our professionals ensure that you are comfortable throughout the procedure.
### Will acne return after treatment?
While treatment can greatly reduce the occurrence of acne, maintaining a proper skincare regimen is essential to prevent future breakouts. For some patients, periodic treatments may be required for ongoing acne control.
### Is acne treatment safe for sensitive skin?
Yes, we offer treatments that are safe and effective for sensitive skin. During your consultation, our experts will recommend a treatment plan that minimizes irritation and maximizes results for your skin type.
