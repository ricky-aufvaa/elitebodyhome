### What are jawline fillers made of?
Most jawline fillers are made of hyaluronic acid, a naturally occurring substance in the body. This helps in achieving defined yet natural-looking enhancements.
### How long do jawline fillers last?
Jawline fillers typically last between 12 to 18 months, depending on the product used and individual metabolism. Periodic touch-ups can help maintain results.
### Is the procedure painful?
Most patients experience minimal discomfort. A topical numbing cream is usually applied to make the procedure as comfortable as possible.
### Are there any side effects?
Common side effects include temporary swelling, bruising, or redness at the injection site. These effects usually resolve within a few days
### Can jawline fillers be reversed?
Yes, if hyaluronic acid-based fillers are used, the results can be reversed using an enzyme called hyaluronidase. This makes the treatment flexible and low-risk.
