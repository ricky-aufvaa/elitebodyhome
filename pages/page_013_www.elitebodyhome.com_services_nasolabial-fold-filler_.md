## Duties to be performed after Nasolabial Fold Filler Treatment
Follow these top aftercare tips for optimal results and a smooth recovery after your nasolabial fold filler treatment:
##### Avoid Touching the Area
Do not touch, massage, or apply pressure to the treated area for at least 24 hours.
##### Minimize Physical Activity
Do not do strenuous activities, such as exercise for 24 to 48 hours after the treatment to minimize your risk of swelling or bruising.
##### Stay Hydrated
Drink lots of water to keep your skin hydrated and even help the filler settle more naturally in the skin.
##### Avoid Heat Exposure
Avoid saunas, steam rooms, and too much sun for a few days, as heat can exacerbate swelling and irritation.
##### Use Cold Compresses
If you have slight swelling or bruising, which is normal, gently hold a cold wrap over the area for 10-15 minutes to ease inflammation.
##### Sleep with a Pillow Under the Chest
For the first night or two, sleep with your head slightly elevated to reduce swelling and to prevent any movement of the filler.
##### Do Not Consume Alcohol and Blood Thinners
Avoid alcohol and blood thinners such as aspirin for at least 24 hours after the procedure to minimize bruising.
