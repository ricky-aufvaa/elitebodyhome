## Frequently Asked Questions
Common questions about 6D Green Laser Treatment
### How many sessions will I need?
Most patients see noticeable results after 4-6 sessions, with maintenance sessions recommended as needed. The exact number depends on your goals and the areas being treated.
### Is the treatment painful?
No, the treatment is completely painless. Patients typically feel only a warm sensation during the procedure.
### How soon will I see results?
Some patients notice changes after the first session, but optimal results typically appear after completing the recommended course of treatments.
### Are there any side effects?
There are typically no side effects, though some patients may experience mild redness or warmth in the treated area that subsides quickly.
### Is the fat reduction permanent?
Yes, the destroyed fat cells are permanently eliminated. However, maintaining a stable weight is important to prevent remaining fat cells from expanding.
### Who is not a good candidate?
This treatment is not suitable for pregnant women, individuals with certain medical conditions, or those with very loose skin.
