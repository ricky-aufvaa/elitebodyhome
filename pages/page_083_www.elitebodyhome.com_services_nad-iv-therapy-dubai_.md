## Frequently Asked Questions
### How long does a NAD IV Therapy session take?
The duration of NAD IV Therapy varies depending on the dosage and individual tolerability, typically ranging between 2-4 hours per session.
### Is NAD IV Therapy painful?
Patients may feel a slight pinch during IV insertion, but the infusion itself is generally painless. Some people report mild warmth or tingling during the treatment.
### How soon will I notice results from NAD IV Therapy?
Many patients report feeling increased energy and mental clarity immediately after treatment, with cumulative benefits becoming more noticeable after several sessions.
### How often should I get NAD IV Therapy?
Frequency depends on individual health goals and conditions. For optimal results, an initial series of 5-10 sessions over 2-4 weeks is often recommended, followed by maintenance sessions.
### Are there any side effects of NAD IV Therapy?
Side effects are typically mild and may include temporary flushing, nausea, or fatigue during the infusion. These usually subside quickly after the treatment.
### Who should avoid NAD IV Therapy?
Individuals with certain medical conditions (like severe kidney disease) or those pregnant/nursing should consult their physician before treatment.
### Can NAD IV Therapy help with addiction recovery?
Yes, NAD IV Therapy has shown promise in reducing withdrawal symptoms and cravings for individuals recovering from substance addiction.
### What's the difference between NAD injections and IV therapy?
IV therapy delivers NAD+ directly into the bloodstream for 100% bioavailability, while injections have lower absorption rates and shorter-lasting effects.
