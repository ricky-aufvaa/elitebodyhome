## What Our Female Fertility Package Includes
This fertility package covers the full range of initial fertility assessments to ongoing monitoring so that women can understand their reproductive health and take proactive steps toward a healthy pregnancy.
## Procedure
The process of assessing your fertility, as well as determining the best treatment, is truly stepwise and patient-specific. Our package involves comprehensive consultations and diagnostic procedures to ensure we identify any issues underlying your fertility and plan the best route to conception.
##### Consultation
An in-depth consultation with a fertility specialist to discuss your medical history, lifestyle, and previous attempts at conceiving.
##### Physical Examination
Broad reproductive health screening to detect problems.
##### Hormonal Profile Testing
Testing hormone levels: Blood tests to measure important fertility hormones such as FSH, LH, and AMH.
##### Ovulation Testing
Tracking and testing to show normal ovulation.

## Preparation
How to Prepare for the Fertility Assessment? Here's what you need to do:
##### Medical Records
Collect and bring any relevant medical history specific to reproductive health, such as past fertility treatments or test results.
##### Track Menstrual Cycle
It can be helpful to track your cycle for three or more months in advance of the consultation in order to offer insights into patterns in ovulation and menstruation.
##### Healthy Lifestyle
Eating a well balanced diet, maintaining a healthy body weight and cutting down on alcohol and caffeine may also help fertility.
