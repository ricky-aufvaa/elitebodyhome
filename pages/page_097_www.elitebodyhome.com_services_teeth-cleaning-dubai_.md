## Frequently Asked Questions
### How often should I get my teeth professionally cleaned?
Most dentists recommend professional teeth cleaning every 6 months, but some people may need more frequent cleanings depending on their oral health.
### Does teeth cleaning hurt?
Teeth cleaning is generally not painful, though you may experience some discomfort if you have sensitive teeth or gums. Our dentists use gentle techniques to minimize any discomfort.
### How long does a teeth cleaning appointment take?
A standard teeth cleaning appointment typically takes between 30-60 minutes, depending on the condition of your teeth and gums.
### Can teeth cleaning whiten my teeth?
While teeth cleaning removes surface stains and can make your teeth appear whiter, it's not the same as professional teeth whitening treatments which use bleaching agents for more dramatic results.
