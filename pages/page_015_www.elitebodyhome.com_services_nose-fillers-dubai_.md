## Aftercare for Nose Filler
So, post-nose filler aftercare is imperative for the best possible results and minimizing side effects. Although the procedure is non-invasive, you can aid your healing and longer-lasting results by following some simple aftercare guidelines. Some post-care tips to follow are:
##### Refrain from Touching or Putting Pressure on the Area
Do not touch, massage or apply pressure to the area for a minimum of 24-48 hours post treatment in order to avoid displacement of filler.
##### Limit Sun Exposure
Swelling or affecting the longevity of the filler can happen when the filler is exposed to direct sun. Put on sunscreen and limit sun exposure for a few days following treatment.
##### Skip Strenuous Activities
If you need to, avoid heavy exercises or allowed excessive sweating for almost one or two days so you would not bother your skin structure.
##### Stay Hydrated
Staying well-hydrated by drinking lots of water will also help to keep your skin hydrated and support healing, which may improve the end result of the filler.
##### Apply Ice Packs for Swelling
If you have minor swelling or bruises, a cold compress will also help reduce inflammation. Do remember to put a cloth between the ice and skin.
##### Avoid Alcohol and Smoking
Both can delay recovery and lead to more swelling or bruising, so it's best to steer clear of them for at least 48 hours after the procedure.
