## Frequently Asked Questions
### How long do the results last?
The longevity varies by procedure. Botox results last 3-4 months, dermal fillers 6-18 months, while laser and microneedling stimulate long-term collagen production for more permanent improvements.
### Is the treatment painful?
Most treatments involve minimal discomfort. Injections may cause slight stinging, while laser and microneedling feel like mild pricking. Topical anesthetics ensure your comfort throughout the procedure.
### Are there any side effects?
Side effects are typically mild and temporary, including slight redness, swelling, or bruising at the treatment site that subsides within a few days.
### How soon can I see results?
Botox shows effects in 3-7 days, fillers provide immediate results, while collagen-stimulating treatments like microneedling show gradual improvement over several weeks.
### Which treatment is best for me?
Our specialists will recommend the optimal treatment after evaluating your skin type, wrinkle depth, and desired results during your consultation.
### Is there any downtime?
Most treatments require minimal to no downtime. You may experience slight redness that typically fades within hours to a couple of days.
