## Frequently Asked Questions
Common questions about male fertility testing
### How long does it take to complete the fertility tests?
The tests included in the Fertility Package for Men typically take 1-2 days to complete, with the results available within a week.
### Can lifestyle affect male fertility?
Yes, lifestyle factors such as smoking, excessive alcohol consumption, and poor diet can significantly impact sperm quality and male fertility.
### What if my semen analysis shows low sperm count?
If the results indicate a low sperm count, our fertility specialists will develop a personalized treatment plan, which may include lifestyle changes, medications, or other fertility treatments.
### Is fertility testing painful?
The tests are generally non-invasive and painless. Semen analysis and blood tests require only sample collection, while ultrasound imaging involves minimal discomfort.
