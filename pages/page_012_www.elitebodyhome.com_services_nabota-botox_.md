## Aftercare Tips for Nabota Botox Treatment
##### Simply going for treatment is not enough. You need a proper aftercare to reduce the side affects and unleash the full results. Below are some of the essential tips that will surely help!
##### Avoid Touching the Treated Area
Do not touch or message the treated area for atleast 24 hours.
##### Stay in a Upper Position
To make sure the Botox is settled in the right way, try to follow an upright position for 4 hours.
##### Avoid Heavy Workouts
Do not exercise for a longer time as it can affect the potential results.
##### No Consuming Alcohol for 24 Hours
Alcohol can increase the risk of bruising. So, not to consume alchol before and after the procedure.
##### Gentle Cleansing
Wash your face gently with a mild cleanser. Do not make any harsh movements or apply creams.
