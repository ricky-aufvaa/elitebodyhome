## Frequently Asked Questions
### How often should I have a general check-up?
Adults are usually advised to have a check-up every year. However, the frequency can vary based on age, lifestyle, and existing health conditions.
### What tests are done during a general check-up?
Basic tests often include blood tests, urine analysis, and screenings for diseases like cancer, diabetes, and heart disease.
### Will I get my test results immediately?
Some tests, like blood pressure and BMI, offer immediate results. Others, such as blood work, may take a few days.
### Can I skip check-ups if I feel healthy?
Even if you feel well, regular check-ups are crucial for detecting hidden conditions that may not present symptoms early on.
