## Mounjaro Side Effects & Restrictions
#### **Mounjaro Common Side Effects:**
  * Nausea
  * Vomiting
  * Diarrhea
  * Decreased appetite
  * Constipation
  * Abdominal discomfort


These effects are usually mild and improve over time as your body adjusts to the medication.
#### **Mounjaro Serious Side Effects (Rare):**
  * Severe allergic reactions (rash, swelling, difficulty breathing)
  * Pancreatitis (severe stomach pain that may radiate to your back)
  * Low blood sugar (dizziness, sweating, shakiness) when used with other diabetes medications
  * Gallbladder issues


#### **Mounjaro Restrictions:**
  * Not for individuals with type 1 diabetes or diabetic ketoacidosis.
  * Avoid if you or your family have a history of medullary thyroid carcinoma or MEN 2.
  * Not recommended for those with severe gastrointestinal disorders like gastroparesis.
  * Consult our doctor by [doctor on call](https://elitebodyhome.com/services/doctor-on-call-dubai/) if pregnant or breastfeeding, as safety is not established.


##  Before Using Mounjaro, Inform Your Healthcare Provider if You:
  * Have a history of pancreatitis or other gastrointestinal issues.
  * Have a history of diabetic retinopathy (eye problems related to diabetes)
  * Have or have had thyroid problems, including thyroid cancer or a family history of thyroid cancer. 
  * Have a history of allergic reactions to medications, especially GLP-1 or GIP receptor agonists.
  * Are pregnant, planning to become pregnant, or breastfeeding.
  * Are taking other medications, including over-the-counter drugs and supplements
  * Have a history of severe gastrointestinal conditions or other medical conditions that may affect your treatment.
