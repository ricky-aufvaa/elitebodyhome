## Procedure for Hyacorp Butt Fillers Treatment
The Hyacorp Butt Filler procedure involves a fast and minimally invasive treatment that is completed via the use of hyaluronic acid to fill and shape the buttock. ##### Filler Injection
A thin needle or cannula is used to inject Hyacorp into the desired locations on your buttocks. This means that the filler must be inserted in the right location in order to increase volume.
##### Sculpting
The injector will shape and sculpt the filler to ensure even distribution and smooth contours.

## Frequently Asked Questions
Got questions about Hyacorp Butt Fillers? Here are some answers to help you feel informed and confident.
### What are Hyacorp butt fillers?
Hyacorp butt fillers are injectable hyaluronic acid-based fillers designed to enhance the volume and contour of the buttocks non-surgically. They offer a temporary alternative to surgical butt augmentation procedures.
### How long do Hyacorp butt fillers last?
The results from Hyacorp fillers can last between 12 to 18 months, depending on factors such as the patient's metabolism, lifestyle, and the amount of filler used.
### Are Hyacorp fillers safe?
Yes, Hyacorp fillers are generally considered safe when administered by a qualified and experienced professional. As with any procedure, there may be minor side effects like swelling or bruising.
### Is there any downtime after the procedure?
Minimal downtime is required. Most patients can resume normal activities within a day or two, although strenuous activities should be avoided for about a week.
### Can Hyacorp fillers be dissolved if I'm not satisfied with the results?
Unlike some facial fillers, Hyacorp is not easily dissolved with hyaluronidase. It is important to have a detailed consultation and realistic expectations before undergoing the treatment.
