## What is Masseter Botox?
Masseter Botox injects botulinum toxin into the masseter muscles in the jaw. These muscles are important for chewing and can become hypertrophied (enlarged) due to overuse, giving you a square or bulky jawline. This is where Masseter Botox comes in ‚Äî it works by relaxing these muscles and allows you to have a more sculpted, harmonious face. It's also widely used to address conditions such as bruxism, which can lead to jaw pain and tension.
## Steps of the Masseter Botox Treatment
##### Consultation
After a thorough consultation to determine your requirements, facial structure, and medical history. The procedure is 100% safe and effective; the specialist will decide whether you are a candidate for Masseter Botox or not.
##### Preparation
Cleaning of the treatment area (jawline) is done, and a numbing cream is applied in some cases to avoid discomfort.
##### Marking the Injection Sites
The specialist will identify the exact spots the Botox will be injected into the masseter muscles and mark them.
##### Botox Injections
Botox is injected into the masseter muscles on both sides of the jaw using a fine needle. The volume of injection is muscle or effect-specific.

## Preparation for Masseter Botox Injection
Getting ready for Masseter Botox is quite easy. The goal is to keep you in peak form for the procedure and minimize bruising or swelling.
##### Avoid blood thinners
Do not take aspirin, ibuprofen or other blood-thinning medications for at least 48 hours before the procedure.
##### Limit alcohol consumption
Stay away from alcohol for at least 24-48 hours prior to treatment as this will minimize the chances of bruising.
##### Discuss medications
Let your provider know whether you take prescription medications, supplements or have health conditions.
##### Stay hydrated
Staying well-hydrated can help your body get ready for the treatment, and also recover afterward.

There's no downtime, and you can continue normal activities right away.

##### Jawline Contouring
You will begin to see some results in 2-4 weeks with the maximum effect at 6 weeks.
##### Symptom Relief
Patients with TMJ or bruxism also feel relief from tension and pain within a week or two after the procedure.
