## Stretch Marks Removal Procedure
The removal of Stretch Marks usually requires a combination of treatments that are targeted to reduce their visibility and improve the texture of the skin.
##### Consultation
The dermatologist evaluates the skin for severity/stage (early red/purple or matured white/silvery) of stretch marks and advises an ideal modality of treatment.
##### Microneedling
Micro needles are applied to the skin to stimulate natural healing and to generate collagen to smooth out and fade stretch marks.
##### Radiofrequency (RF) Therapy
The RF energy tightens and firms the overlying skin, inducing collagen and elastin production, causing the stretch marks to fade and the skin to tighten.
##### Chemical Peels
Special peels peel the surface of the skin, reducing the look of stretch marks and improving the quality of the skin.
##### Therapeutic PRP
PRP harnesses the power of the patient's own blood to create a high concentration of growth factors and promote tissue renewal.
##### Fractional Laser
Targets deep into the skin's dermal layer to increase collagen and heal the damaged skin, which reduces both new and old marks.
##### Follow-Up & Aftercare
Use of good skincare such as moisturizer and sunblock are important to achieve the best long-term results.
