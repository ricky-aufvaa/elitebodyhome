Root canal treatment, or endodontic therapy, is a dental standard aiming to fix or save severely damaged, infected, or decaying teeth. By removing the infected pulp from inside the tooth, cleaning and disinfecting the root canal, and sealing it with a filling or crown, this procedure can relieve pain, preserve your natural teeth and restore full functionality to your smile.

## 10 Signs You May Need a Root Canal Treatment
Are you asking yourself if you need a root canal? Here are some of its more common symptoms: Ongoing toothache or sharp illness. A sensitivity to hot or cold temperatures. Swelling or tenderness in the gums around the affected tooth. The tooth is darkening or changed color. Little pimple-like bumps on the gums. Failure to heed these signs may result in serious infections, abscesses or tooth loss.
## Complete Procedure for Root Canal Treatment in Dubai
##### Consultation
Our endodontist in Dubai will do a comprehensive physical examination during the first appointment and perform X-ray tests to evaluate damage and suggest the ideal treatment plan.
##### Anesthesia
The entire process is done under local anesthesia, thereby making it absolutely painless.
##### Cleaning & Shaping the Canal
Then the infected pulp is extracted, and modern instruments are used to clean and shape the root canal.
##### Filling & Sealing
A biocompatible material is then packed into the cleaned root canal to prevent reinfection.
##### Restoration
To strengthen, restore function, and improve the appearance of the tooth, a dental crown is used to cover it.
##### Follow-Up
You will have follow-up care and recovery monitoring from our team.
## Reasons to Keep Your Natural Teeth with Root Canal Therapy
By having a root canal, you can avoid extracting and replacing with an artificial tooth, as it allows you to save your natural teeth.
##### Preventative for Jawbone Loss
Root canal therapy helps maintain your natural tooth structure, preventing bone loss in the jaw and supporting long-term oral health.
##### Preserving Chewing and Speaking Function
Saving your natural teeth ensures you can continue chewing comfortably and speaking clearly without the need for dentures or implants.
##### Avoids Misalignment of Surrounding Teeth
By keeping your natural tooth in place, root canal treatment prevents neighboring teeth from shifting out of alignment and affecting your bite.
##### Long-Lasting Results with Good Care
A tooth treated with a root canal can last a lifetime with proper oral hygiene and regular dental checkups, offering a reliable, cost-effective solution.

## Post-Operative Care Guidelines for Root Canal
##### Avoid chewing on treated tooth
Do not chew on the treated tooth until it is completely restored.
##### Pain management
Treat discomfort with over-the-counter pain relievers.
##### Maintain oral hygiene
Brush and floss regularly to keep teeth clean.
##### Follow-up appointments
Get follow-up appointments as they will help heal properly.
##### Avoid certain foods
Refrain from sticky or hard foods to prevent damaging the tooth.
