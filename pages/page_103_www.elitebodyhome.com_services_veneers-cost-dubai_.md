## What Are Veneers?
Veneers are thin, custom-made shells that cover the front surface of teeth to improve their appearance. Porcelain or composite resin forms, they rectify discoloration, chips, gaps, and minor misalignment. Veneers offer a natural, durable solution for a perfect smile with minimal alteration of the tooth.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### How long do veneers typically last?
Porcelain veneers last 10-15 years, while composite veneers last 5-7 years with proper care and regular dental check-ups.
### Are veneers a permanent solution?
Veneers are semi-permanent since some enamel is removed. While long-lasting, they may need replacement over time due to wear or damage.
### Can veneers fix crooked teeth?
Veneers can correct mild misalignment, but severe cases may require orthodontic treatments like braces or Invisalign for proper alignment.
### Do veneers stain over time?
Porcelain veneers are stain-resistant, but composite veneers may discolor.
