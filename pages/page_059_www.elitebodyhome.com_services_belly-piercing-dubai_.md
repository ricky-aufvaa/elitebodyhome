## Aftercare and Healing
In order to avoid complications, it is essential to have proper aftercare so that your belly piercing heals perfectly. To heal best, do the following:
##### Twice Daily Clean the Piercing
Clean the area with saline or a mild, fragrance-free soap.
##### Avoid Touching the Piercing
Touch your piercing only with clean hands to avoid introducing bacteria into the wound.
##### Be Careful with Clothing
Steer clear of tight-fitting clothes that might rub against the piercing and irritate it.
##### Do Not Soak the Piercing in Water
Avoid baths, hot tubs, and swimming until fully healed.
