## Frequently Asked Questions
### How long does the treatment take?
The treatment typically lasts 30 to 60 minutes, depending on the size of the area being treated.
### Is Radiofrequency Microneedling painful?
Most patients experience minimal discomfort during the procedure. A topical numbing cream is applied beforehand to reduce any potential discomfort.
### How many sessions are required?
Most patients need 3 to 4 sessions, spaced about a month apart, for optimal results. Your provider will create a customized treatment plan based on your skin concerns.
### What is the downtime after treatment?
Downtime is minimal, with most patients experiencing redness and mild swelling for 1 to 2 days post-treatment. You can usually resume normal activities after 24 hours.
