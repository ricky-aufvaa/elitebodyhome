## Aftercare Tips
After a laser beard treatment, there may be some mild redness or tingling, but this typically resolves within hours. To enhance your outcome:

Wear sunscreen and avoid direct sun exposure for at least a week.
##### Gentle Skin Care

If necessary, cold compresses can be applied to the treated area to relieve discomfort.
##### Hydration
Moisten the area while it heals.
