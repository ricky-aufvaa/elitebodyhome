The follow-up care needed after Botox includes avoiding strenuous exercise, not lying down for at least four hours, and steering clear of excessive sun exposure or heat for 24 hours to ensure optimal results and minimize side effects.
Are there any contraindications for underarm botox?
The contraindications for underarm Botox include pregnancy, breastfeeding, certain neuromuscular disorders, allergies to any botulinum toxin products, and current infections at the injection site. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before treatment.
How does botox work to reduce sweating?
Botox works to reduce sweating by blocking the signals from the nerves that stimulate sweat glands. This effectively inhibits excessive sweat production in targeted areas, providing relief for those with conditions like hyperhidrosis.
