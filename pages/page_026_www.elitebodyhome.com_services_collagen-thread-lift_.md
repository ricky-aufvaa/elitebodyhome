## Q-What is a Collagen Thread Lift?
A Collagen Thread Lift is a type of non-surgical cosmetic procedure that uses dissolvable threads (usually made of collagen or polydioxanone (PDO) to lift and tighten sagging skin. These threads are placed in tissue beneath the surface of the skin and assist in stimulating the body's natural collagen generators, enhancing skin flexibility and tightness. This can give patients a refreshed appearance without undergoing more invasive surgical corporals. The threads dissolve gradually over time, but the boost of collagen helps keep those results for months.
## Process For Collagen Thread Lift Procedure
The Collagen Thread Lift procedure works by inserting dissolvable threads under the skin and lifts the sagging areas by tightening the skin. These threads are made of biocompatible materials, like polydioxanone (PDO), which dissolve on their own over time, but also induce collagen proliferation.
##### Consultation
A thorough consultation with the doctor to evaluate your skin, establish treatment goals, and describe the procedure.
##### Preparation
The area is cleaned and a topical anesthetic is applied to reduce any discomfort.
##### Thread Insertion
Collagen-stimulating threads are delicately implanted under the skin through a thin needle or cannula. These threads provide scaffolding to lift sagging skin.
##### Collagen Stimulation
The threads promote collagen production over the next period, providing natural rejuvenation of your skin and firmness.
##### Positioning & Lifting
The stitches are then delicately manipulated to elevate and smoothen the skin, sculpting better-defined contours.
##### Completion
The threads dissolve slowly, resulting in firmer, more youthful-looking skin.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### What areas can be treated with a Collagen Thread Lift?
Common areas treated include the cheeks, jawline, brow, neck, and under-eye region. This procedure can also be used to lift and contour specific areas such as the nose and eyebrows.
### Is the procedure painful?
The procedure is relatively painless due to the use of local anesthesia. Patients typically report feeling some pressure or mild discomfort, but not significant pain.
### How long do the results last?
Results from a Collagen Thread Lift can last between 12-18 months, depending on factors such as age, lifestyle, and skin type. Since the threads stimulate collagen production, the skin continues to improve even after the threads dissolve.
### Are there any side effects?
Common side effects include mild swelling, bruising, or tenderness in the treated area, which typically subsides within a few days. In rare cases, more serious complications such as infection or visible threads may occur, though these are uncommon.
