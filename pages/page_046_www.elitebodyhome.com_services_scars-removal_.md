## Frequently Asked Questions
### How many treatments are needed?
The number of treatments varies depending on the type of scar and the chosen procedure. Some patients see results after 1-3 sessions, while others may require additional sessions for deeper scars.
### Does scar removal hurt?
Most treatments are associated with minimal discomfort. Numbing creams are often used to ensure the procedure is as painless as possible.
### Are results permanent?
Results are generally long-lasting, but some scars may not disappear completely. Maintenance treatments might be necessary for optimal results, especially for keloids or hypertrophic scars.
### What are the risks?
Potential side effects can include redness, swelling, or temporary skin sensitivity. Our team takes every precaution to minimize risks.
