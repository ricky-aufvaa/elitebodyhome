## What is Hyperpigmentation?
Hyperpigmentation is nothing more than the excessive generation of the melanin pigment that is formed in that skin and which generates a darkening effect. It can also present as dark spots, patches or discoloration wherever we get the most sun exposure ‚Äî the face, neck, hands and shoulders. The condition may be induced by hormonal fluctuations, exposure of the skin to sunlight, some medications, or by an injury to the skin. Hyperpigmentation is sometimes initiated from birth or it can also be developed later on in life due to acne, scar tissue, or any environmental factor.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### How much does hyperpigmentation treatment in Dubai cost?
The cost depends on your type of skin, the number of sittings you'll need to attend, and the treatment.
### Is hyperpigmentation permanent?
In most cases, hyperpigmentation fades with the right treatment. However, some forms can be permanent or take a long time to fade, especially if not treated early.
### How long does hyperpigmentation take to fade?
Depending on your skin condition and the treatment, you can see results in a few weeks to several months.
### What treatments remove hyperpigmentation?
Chemical peels, laser treatments, and microneedling are some of the most effective ways to remove hyperpigmentation.
### Which treatment is best for hyperpigmentation?
This depends on your skin type, pigmentation level, and cause. Our dermatologists can help determine the best treatment for you.
### Can hyperpigmentation be removed naturally?
Some natural remedies may help lighten pigmentation, but they are less effective than clinical treatments and take longer to show results.
### Can laser remove hyperpigmentation permanently?
Laser treatment can significantly reduce or remove hyperpigmentation. However, new pigmentation can form due to sun exposure or other factors, so maintenance is key.
