## Comprehensive Keloid Treatment Options
Keloid treatment involves a customized approach based on scar size, location, and severity to effectively reduce raised tissue and prevent recurrence.We offer a range of advanced treatments tailored to your specific keloid condition:
##### Corticosteroid Injections
Reduce inflammation and gradually flatten keloids through a series of injections that target collagen overproduction.
##### Cryotherapy
Freeze and destroy keloid tissue using controlled cold temperatures, particularly effective for smaller keloids.
##### Laser Therapy
Advanced laser technology reduces redness, improves texture, and helps flatten raised scar tissue with precision.
##### Surgical Excision
Surgical removal of keloid tissue, often combined with other therapies to minimize recurrence risk.
##### Radiation Therapy
Used post-surgery to significantly reduce the chance of keloid regrowth in stubborn cases.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### Can keloids come back after treatment?
While recurrence is possible with keloids, combination therapies significantly reduce this risk. Our specialists employ advanced techniques and follow-up care to minimize the chance of regrowth.
### Is keloid treatment painful?
Most treatments involve minimal discomfort. We use appropriate anesthesia and pain management techniques to ensure your comfort throughout the procedure.
### How many treatment sessions are typically needed?
The number of sessions varies based on keloid size, location, and response to treatment. Most patients require multiple sessions spaced several weeks apart for optimal results.
### Are the results permanent?
With proper treatment and aftercare, results are long-lasting. However, keloids have a tendency to recur, so maintenance sessions may be recommended for certain cases.
