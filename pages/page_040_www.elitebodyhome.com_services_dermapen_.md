## What is Dermapen?
Dermapen is an advanced micro-needling device that creates controlled micro-injuries on the surface of the skin to stimulate the body's natural healing mechanism. This causes the body to produce collagen and elastin, which aid in the repair of skin damage and the reduction of prevalent skin conditions such as wrinkles, pimple scarring, and pigmentation. The Dermapen is small and precise, allowing for an effective delivery in targeted areas of the face and body.

## Preparation
##### Dermapen Microneedling aftercare: Prepping your Skin Before Treatment
Preparing your skin beforehand is an important step to ensure your dermapen microneedling treatment will give you the best results, and to ensure a smooth recovery. Here are steps you can take in advance of your appointment.
##### Avoid Sun Exposure
Minimize sun exposure and do not tan for at least one week before treatment.
##### Stop Using Retinoids
Do not use retinoid creams or any exfoliating products for 3 to 5 days before the procedure.
##### Hydrate Your Skin
In the days leading up to treatment, ensure that your skin is well moisturized.
##### Avoid Blood Thinners
Refrain from taking aspirin, ibuprofen or any blood-thinning medications, if you can, to minimize bruising.
##### Consultation
Your provider will ask you about any medications and skin conditions that may impact your treatment during your consultation.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### How soon will I see results?
Results vary, but most patients begin to see improvement in hair texture and reduction in shedding after the first few treatments. Significant regrowth can be seen after 3 to 6 months of continuous therapy.
### Is the procedure painful?
The injections can cause mild discomfort, but most patients find it tolerable. A numbing cream can be applied to minimize any pain.
### How many sessions will I need?
Typically, 3 to 6 sessions are recommended, spaced about 4 to 6 weeks apart, for optimal results. Your clinician will create a personalized treatment plan based on your hair loss condition.
### Are there any side effects?
The treatment is generally safe, with minimal side effects such as mild swelling, redness, or bruising at the injection site. These usually subside within a few days.
