## Frequently Asked Questions
### Is IV drip therapy at home safe?
Yes, IV drip therapy at home is safe when performed by certified healthcare professionals. In Dubai, licensed nurses or doctors administer the treatment, ensuring proper sterilization and protocol adherence.
### How long does an IV drip session last?
An IV drip session typically lasts between 30 to 60 minutes, depending on the type of infusion and your health needs.
### What types of IV drips are available?
There are various formulations available, including hydration drips, immune-boosting drips, energy boosters, anti-aging drips, and drips tailored to alleviate jet lag, migraines, or hangovers.
### How often can I get IV drip therapy?
The frequency of your sessions depends on your health goals and the recommendation of your healthcare provider. Some people opt for weekly sessions, while others may choose monthly treatments.
### Can everyone use IV therapy?
IV therapy is safe for most people, but a healthcare provider will assess your eligibility based on your medical history and current health status. Those with certain medical conditions or allergies should consult with their doctor before starting IV therapy.
