## What is the PRP Biotin Hair Loss Treatment?
It combines the power of PRP (platelet-rich plasma) with biotin, an essential vitamin for hair health. Whether you're dealing with thinning hair, male or female pattern baldness, or hair shedding, this treatment can be a minimally invasive, natural way to revive hair growth.
## Procedure
The Biotin PRP (Platelet Rich Plasma) Hair Loss Treatment is a combination of two highly effective methods; both the extraction of PRP from your own blood and the infusion of biotin, which creates a powerhouse mixture capable of feeding your scalp. With this technique hair follicles are rejuvenated, maintains blood circulation and increases density.
##### Consultation
Biotin PRP starts with a consultation with a specialist who assesses your hair loss issues and ascertains whether this treatment is suitable for you.
##### Blood Draw
Only a few milliliters of blood are drawn and placed into a PRP tube with biotin for regenerative effects.
##### PRP Preparation
The blood is spun in a centrifuge so that platelet-rich plasma (PRP) becomes separated from the rest of the components, thus achieving a high concentration.
##### Scalp Injection
PRP With Biotin is then injected into the areas of the scalp where hair thinning or loss is being experienced.
##### Biotin Infusion
PRP is fortified with biotin, a vitamin that helps your hair grow healthily.
##### Recovery & Results
The PRP acts on hair follicles over time to promote natural hair and designed to thicken. For best results, multiple sessions may be recommended.
