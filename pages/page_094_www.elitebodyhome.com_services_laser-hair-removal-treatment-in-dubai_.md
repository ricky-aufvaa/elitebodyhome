## Steps in Laser Hair Removal Treatment
Laser hair removal is a procedure that uses concentrated light beams that dive into the unwanted hair and destroys its roots. The hair pigment absorbs the laser energy, which destroys the follicle's state of new hair production and then the hair growth is greatly reduced.
##### Laser Targets Hair Follicles
The laser releases light energy that is absorbed by the melanin in the hair, heating it and destroying the follicle.
##### Precision and Control
The treatment targets hair through deep penetration but leaves surrounding skin tissue unharmed.
##### Multiple Sessions Required
Because hair grows in cycles, multiple treatments are necessary to affect the follicles at various points in their growth stages.
##### Cooling Technology
Modern lasers are equipped with cooling systems to lessen pain during the procedure.

## Aftercare Tips
Laser hair removal is an excellent choice for people who want to be hair-free, but taking care of your skin afterward is extremely important to ensure it not only looks great but also gets the most benefits from the treatment.
##### Avoid Sun Exposure
Avoid direct sunlight on the treated area for at least two weeks.
##### Skip Heat Treatments
Avoid hot showers, saunas and steam rooms for at least 48 hours after treatment.
##### Use Soothing Products
You can also apply a cool compress for soothing or an aloe vera gel to wipe away any redness or discomfort on the treated area.
##### Gentle Skincare
And for a week, you should steer clear of any harsh exfoliating products or scrubs on the treated area.
