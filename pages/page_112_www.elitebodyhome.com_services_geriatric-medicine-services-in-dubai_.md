## Frequently Asked Questions
Common questions about geriatric medicine
### At what age should I start seeing a geriatric specialist?
We recommend consulting a geriatrician around age 65 or earlier if you have complex medical needs.
### What conditions do geriatricians treat?
Geriatricians manage a wide range of conditions, including arthritis, dementia, osteoporosis, diabetes, and cardiovascular diseases.
### Is geriatric medicine covered by insurance?
Many insurance plans cover geriatric care. Check with your provider for details about coverage.
### How often should seniors visit a geriatrician?
Regular visits are recommended every 6-12 months, or more frequently if managing chronic conditions.
