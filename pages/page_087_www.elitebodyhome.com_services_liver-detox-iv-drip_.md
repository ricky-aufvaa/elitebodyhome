Common questions about Liver Detox IV Therapy
### How often should I get a Liver Detox IV Drip?
For optimal results, we recommend starting with 1-2 sessions per week for 3-4 weeks, then monthly maintenance sessions. Your practitioner will customize a plan based on your needs.
### Is the treatment painful?
Most patients experience minimal discomfort - just a small pinch during IV insertion. The treatment itself is relaxing and pain-free.
### How quickly will I see results?
Many patients report feeling more energetic within hours. Full benefits typically develop over several days as toxins are processed and eliminated.
### Are there any side effects?
Side effects are rare but may include mild bruising at the injection site or temporary fatigue as your body processes toxins. These resolve quickly.
### Can I combine this with other detox methods?
Yes, our Liver Detox IV complements other detox approaches like clean eating, exercise, and sauna therapy. We can create a comprehensive detox plan for you.
### Is this treatment safe for everyone?
While generally safe, we recommend consulting with our medical team if you have severe liver conditions or are pregnant. We customize each treatment based on health history.
