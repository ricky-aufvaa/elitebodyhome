Arrive with clean skin free of makeup, lotions or skincare products.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### How long do Stylage Fillers last?
Stylage Fillers typically last between 9 to 12 months, but in some cases, results may last up to 18 months, depending on the specific filler and treated area.
### Are there any side effects?
Mild swelling, redness, and bruising are the most common side effects. These usually resolve within a few days. Thanks to the antioxidant mannitol, post-procedural inflammation is minimized.
### How soon will I see results?
Results are visible immediately after the treatment, though minor swelling may temporarily obscure the final outcome. Once swelling subsides (within 1-3 days), the full effects can be appreciated.
### Can Stylage Fillers be combined with other treatments?
Yes, Stylage Fillers can be combined with other cosmetic treatments such as Botox, microneedling, or chemical peels for more comprehensive facial rejuvenation.
