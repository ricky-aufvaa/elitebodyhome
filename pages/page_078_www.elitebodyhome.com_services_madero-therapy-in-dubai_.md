## What is Madero Therapy and How Does it Work?
Madero therapy (also known as wood therapy) is a holistic massage technique that uses wooden instruments designed for body contouring and sculpting. The process involves rolling wooden rollers and cups on legs, belly, and back to apply pressure. This therapy breaks down localized fat, smoothens cellulite and improves skin elasticity with consistent application.
## Madero's Therapy: Procedure of Each Step
For effective results, Madero therapy in Dubai encompasses a series of steps:
##### Consultation
Our specialists discuss your body goals/concerns and observe the required results.
##### Preparation
You lie down comfortably on the treatment table while our therapist applies natural oil to prepare the skin.
##### Therapy Session
Various wooden tools target specific areas with gentle pressure for lymphatic drainage and fat breakdown.
##### Duration
Each session lasts 60 minutes. Results can typically be seen within a few sessions.
